# Rwanda Tourist Experience & Local Guide Booking Platform - SRS

## Mission
Connect travelers with experiences and local guides they can trust, supporting businesses and the Rwandan tourism sector.

## Problem Statement
Informal booking systems (WhatsApp, social media) are unreliable. There is a gap between tourist demand and verified guide supply.

## User Roles
- **Tourist**: Local/International traveler seeking experiences.
- **Local Guide**: Verified professional with a profile, calendar, and specific skills.
- **Platform Administrator**: Manages verification, disputes, and reporting.

## Key Features (Sprint 1-5)
- **Sprint 1**: Auth & Profiles (OTP, Google OAuth, Guide Profiles).
- **Sprint 2**: Search & Discovery (Filtering, Google Maps).
- **Sprint 3**: Booking & Payments (MTN/Airtel MoMo, Cards).
- **Sprint 4**: Reviews & Admin Dashboard.
- **Sprint 5**: Testing & Hardening.

## Technical Constraints
- 3G optimization (3s load time).
- 99.5% uptime.
- Verified Guide IDs.
- Responsive design (320px to 1920px).
